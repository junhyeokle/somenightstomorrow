somenightstomorrow

A personal platform for preserving projects, memories, experiments, and growth.

⸻

What is this?

This project began with a simple goal: building my own website.

But somewhere along the way, it became something much bigger.

This website is a place to preserve who I was, what I built, what I loved, and what I learned.

It records my projects, my thoughts, my favorite films, music, games, books, and the journey behind them.

More than anything, it is a place where my future self can come back and remember who I was today.

⸻

Why?

I don’t want to remember only the results.

I want to remember the process.

I want to remember what I was thinking, what I struggled with, what inspired me, and what kind of person I was becoming.

This project exists so that one day I can look back and say,

“So this is who I was.”

⸻

Current Status

I still feel like I’m somewhere in the middle of a forest.

I know where I want to go, but I haven’t fully figured out where I am yet.

Right now, I’m not trying to walk faster.

I’m trying to understand the map before taking the next step.

⸻

Where is this project going?

Before reaching the destination, I first need to understand my current position.

Then I need to find the path.

Only after that can I keep moving forward.

This website will continue to grow together with me.

It won’t just document completed projects.

It will document the journey toward them.
